Concordia University GPA Calculator!

Use the form below to add your courses and grades. The calculator will automatically display your cumulative GPA, as well as the GPA for each class that you entered.

Add Courses

Acceptable inputs are as follows:

  • Course Name: Any course name is allowed
  • Credits: A real number greater than or equal to 0, and less than or equal to 15 (ex: 4.5 or 3.0)
  • Grade: Letter grades A, B, C, D, and F. Plus and minuses are allowed (ex: B+ or A-)
